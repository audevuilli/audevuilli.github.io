About

I am a food scientist, urban analyst and environmental researcher. My interest lies in the culture of food, the influence of urban environment and social interaction on human health, wellbeing and education. I use advanced data science tools and train myself to champion emerging technologies.

Passionate about research and innovation, I studied a BSc. in Food Science at ETH Zurich, and worked at GrowUp Urban Farm, one of the first commercial vertical farm in London. I pursued with a MSc. in Smart Cities and Urban Analytics at The Bartlett Center for Advanced Spatial Analysis at UCL, undertaking my MSc. thesis at Movement Strategies. I recently interned at the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD) as a data analyst. Now, I am looking to starting a PhD that I name GardensofThings at UCL.
